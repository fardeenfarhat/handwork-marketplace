## Step-by-Step Onboarding

### Step 1: Download and Install

1. **Find the App**
   - **iOS**: Open App Store, search "Handwork Marketplace"
   - **Android**: Open Google Play Store, search "Handwork Marketplace"

2. **Install**
   - Tap "Install" or "Get"
   - Wait for download to complete
   - Tap the app icon to open

### Step 2: Create Your Account

1. **Choose Sign-Up Method**
   - **Email**: Enter email and create password
   - **Social Login**: Use Google, Facebook, or Apple
   - **Phone**: Sign up with phone number

2. **Select Your Role**
   - **Client**: I need to hire workers for jobs
   - **Worker**: I want to find work opportunities
   - **Both**: I might hire workers and also work for others

3. **Basic Information**
   - First and last name
   - Phone number (if not used for signup)
   - Location (city, state)
   - Date of birth

### Step 3: Verify Your Account

1. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link
   - Return to the app

2. **Phone Verification**
   - Enter the SMS code sent to your phone
   - If you don't receive it, tap "Resend"
   - Contact support if issues persist

### Step 4: Complete Your Profile

#### For All Users

1. **Profile Photo**
   - Tap the camera icon
   - Choose "Take Photo" or "Choose from Library"
   - Crop and adjust as needed
   - Use a clear, professional headshot

2. **Basic Information**
   - Write a brief bio (2-3 sentences)
   - Add your location details
   - Set your availability preferences
   - Choose notification settings

#### For Workers Only

1. **Skills and Services**
   - Select your primary categories:
     - Plumbing
     - Electrical
     - Cleaning
     - Construction
     - HVAC
     - Landscaping
     - And more...
   - Add specific skills within each category
   - Include any certifications or licenses

2. **Portfolio**
   - Upload 3-5 high-quality photos of your work
   - Show before/after shots when possible
   - Include different types of projects
   - Add captions describing each project

3. **Pricing**
   - Set your hourly rate
   - Research local market rates
   - Consider your experience level
   - You can adjust this anytime

4. **KYC Verification** (Required)
   - Upload government-issued ID
   - Provide proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
   - Take a selfie for identity verification
   - Wait for approval (usually 24-48 hours)

#### For Clients Only

1. **Payment Method**
   - Add a credit/debit card
   - Or connect PayPal account
   - This enables you to hire workers
   - Your payment info is secure and encrypted

2. **Preferences**
   - Set your typical budget ranges
   - Choose preferred communication methods
   - Select notification preferences
   - Add any special requirements

### Step 5: Learn the Platform

1. **Take the Tutorial**
   - Interactive walkthrough of key features
   - Learn how to navigate the app
   - Understand the hiring/application process
   - Practice using messaging features

2. **Read Safety Guidelines**
   - Personal safety tips
   - Platform policies
   - How to report issues
   - Emergency procedures

3. **Explore Features**
   - Browse jobs or workers
   - Try the search and filter functions
   - Check out the messaging system
   - Review the payment process

## Your First Week

### For Workers

#### Day 1-2: Set Up for Success
- Complete all profile sections
- Upload quality portfolio images
- Research competitive rates in your area
- Read job posting guidelines

#### Day 3-4: Start Applying
- Browse available jobs in your categories
- Apply to 3-5 jobs that match your skills
- Write personalized application messages
- Follow up on applications after 24 hours

#### Day 5-7: Build Momentum
- Respond quickly to client messages
- Be flexible with your first few jobs
- Focus on delivering excellent work
- Ask satisfied clients for reviews

### For Clients

#### Day 1-2: Prepare Your First Job
- Think about what work you need done
- Take photos of the area/problem
- Research typical costs for the work
- Write a clear job description

#### Day 3-4: Post and Manage
- Post your first job
- Review worker applications as they come in
- Ask questions to clarify proposals
- Check worker profiles and reviews

#### Day 5-7: Complete Your First Hire
- Select the best worker for your job
- Communicate clearly about expectations
- Monitor progress and provide feedback
- Leave a review after completion

## Common First-Week Challenges

### For Workers

**Challenge**: "I'm not getting any responses to my applications"
**Solutions**:
- Improve your profile completeness
- Write more personalized application messages
- Apply to jobs quickly after they're posted
- Consider adjusting your rates
- Ask for feedback from the support team

**Challenge**: "I don't know how to price my services"
**Solutions**:
- Research local market rates online
- Start with competitive rates to build reviews
- Factor in travel time and materials
- Ask other workers in your network
- Adjust based on demand and experience

### For Clients

**Challenge**: "I'm not getting quality applications"
**Solutions**:
- Make your job description more detailed
- Increase your budget range if too low
- Add photos to show the work needed
- Respond quickly to good applications
- Consider the timing of your post

**Challenge**: "I'm not sure how to evaluate workers"
**Solutions**:
- Focus on ratings and recent reviews
- Look for relevant experience in their portfolio
- Ask specific questions about your job
- Check their response time and communication
- Trust your instincts about professionalism

## Tips for Success

### Universal Tips
1. **Complete Your Profile**: Profiles with photos and complete information get 3x more engagement
2. **Respond Quickly**: Fast response times improve your success rate
3. **Communicate Clearly**: Clear communication prevents misunderstandings
4. **Be Professional**: Treat every interaction as a business relationship
5. **Use the App Features**: In-app messaging, payments, and reviews protect both parties

### Worker-Specific Tips
1. **Start Competitive**: Price competitively for your first few jobs to build reviews
2. **Show Your Work**: High-quality portfolio photos are crucial
3. **Be Reliable**: Always show up on time and complete work as promised
4. **Ask for Reviews**: Politely ask satisfied clients to leave reviews
5. **Expand Gradually**: Add new skills and services as you gain experience

### Client-Specific Tips
1. **Be Detailed**: Clear job descriptions attract better workers
2. **Set Realistic Budgets**: Research typical costs for your type of work
3. **Hire Quickly**: Good workers get booked fast
4. **Provide Feedback**: Help workers do their best work with clear guidance
5. **Build Relationships**: Great workers often become repeat service providers
