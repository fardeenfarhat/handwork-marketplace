## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Download the App**
   - iOS: Search "Handwork Marketplace" in the App Store
   - Android: Search "Handwork Marketplace" in Google Play Store

2. **Sign Up**
   - Open the app and tap "Sign Up"
   - Choose your role: Client or Worker
   - Enter your email, phone number, and create a password
   - Or sign up quickly with Google, Facebook, or Apple

3. **Verify Your Account**
   - Check your email for a verification link
   - Verify your phone number with the SMS code
   - Complete your profile setup

### Profile Setup

**For All Users:**
- Add a profile photo
- Fill in your basic information
- Set your location
- Add a brief bio

**Additional for Workers:**
- Upload portfolio images
- List your skills and services
- Set your hourly rate
- Complete KYC verification (ID and address proof)

## For Clients

### Posting a Job

1. **Create a Job Post**
   - Tap the "+" button on the home screen
   - Select "Post a Job"
   - Choose the appropriate category (plumbing, electrical, cleaning, etc.)

2. **Job Details**
   - Write a clear, descriptive title
   - Provide detailed description of the work needed
   - Set your budget range
   - Add your location
   - Select preferred date and time
   - Upload photos if helpful

3. **Review and Post**
   - Review all details
   - Tap "Post Job" to make it live
   - Your job will be visible to qualified workers

### Managing Applications

1. **Receiving Applications**
   - You'll get notifications when workers apply
   - View applications in the "My Jobs" section
   - Review worker profiles, ratings, and proposals

2. **Evaluating Workers**
   - Check their ratings and reviews
   - Look at their portfolio and experience
   - Read their application message
   - Compare proposed rates and timelines

3. **Hiring a Worker**
   - Select the best candidate
   - Tap "Hire" on their application
   - Confirm the agreed rate and start date
   - Payment will be held in escrow

### During the Job

1. **Communication**
   - Use in-app messaging to coordinate
   - Share additional details or photos
   - Confirm arrival times and access instructions

2. **Monitoring Progress**
   - Track job status updates
   - Receive notifications of progress
   - Worker will update status to "In Progress" when starting

3. **Job Completion**
   - Worker will mark job as "Completed"
   - Review completion photos
   - Approve completion if satisfied
   - Payment is automatically released

### After Job Completion

1. **Leave a Review**
   - Rate the worker (1-5 stars)
   - Write a detailed review
   - This helps other clients and improves the platform

2. **Payment Receipt**
   - You'll receive a payment confirmation
   - Download receipt from the app
   - Payment history is available in your profile

## For Workers

### Finding Jobs

1. **Browse Available Jobs**
   - View jobs on the home screen
   - Use filters to find relevant work:
     - Category (your skills)
     - Location (distance from you)
     - Budget range
     - Date posted

2. **Job Recommendations**
   - Our AI suggests jobs based on your skills and location
   - Check the "Recommended" tab for personalized matches
   - Enable notifications for new job alerts

### Applying for Jobs

1. **Review Job Details**
   - Read the full job description
   - Check location and travel requirements
   - Review client's rating and history
   - Assess if the job matches your skills

2. **Submit Your Application**
   - Write a personalized message
   - Highlight relevant experience
   - Propose your rate (can be within or outside budget range)
   - Suggest your availability
   - Submit application

3. **Application Status**
   - Track application status in "My Applications"
   - Receive notifications for updates
   - Client may message you for clarification

### Getting Hired

1. **Acceptance Notification**
   - You'll be notified when hired
   - Review final job details and agreed rate
   - Confirm your availability
   - Accept the booking

2. **Pre-Job Communication**
   - Message the client to confirm details
   - Ask about access, parking, tools needed
   - Confirm arrival time
   - Share your contact information if needed

### Completing Jobs

1. **Starting Work**
   - Update job status to "In Progress" when you arrive
   - Take before photos if relevant
   - Communicate any issues or changes needed

2. **During Work**
   - Keep client updated on progress
   - Ask questions if anything is unclear
   - Document your work with photos

3. **Finishing Up**
   - Take completion photos
   - Update status to "Completed"
   - Add completion notes
   - Clean up work area

### Getting Paid

1. **Payment Process**
   - Client reviews and approves completion
   - Payment is automatically released from escrow
   - You'll receive notification of payment
   - Funds appear in your account within 1-2 business days

2. **Withdrawal**
   - Go to "Earnings" in your profile
   - Tap "Withdraw Funds"
   - Choose your preferred method (bank transfer, PayPal)
   - Minimum withdrawal: $50

### Building Your Reputation

1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add professional photos of your work
   - List all relevant skills and certifications
   - Keep your availability updated
   - Respond quickly to messages

2. **Deliver Quality Work**
   - Arrive on time
   - Communicate clearly
   - Complete work as agreed
   - Go above and beyond when possible

3. **Collect Reviews**
   - Ask satisfied clients to leave reviews
   - Maintain high ratings (4.5+ stars)
   - Address any negative feedback professionally

## Payments

### How Payments Work

1. **Escrow System**
   - Client pays upfront when hiring
   - Funds are held securely in escrow
   - Released to worker upon job completion
   - Protects both parties

2. **Payment Methods**
   - Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
   - PayPal
   - Bank transfers
   - Apple Pay / Google Pay

### Pricing and Fees

1. **Platform Fees**
   - Workers: 10% service fee on earnings
   - Clients: 3% processing fee on payments
   - No hidden fees or charges

2. **Pricing Guidelines**
   - Research market rates in your area
   - Consider job complexity and duration
   - Factor in travel time and materials
   - Be competitive but fair

### Dispute Resolution

1. **If Issues Arise**
   - Try to resolve directly with the other party
   - Use in-app messaging for documentation
   - Contact support if needed
   - Funds remain in escrow during disputes

2. **Dispute Process**
   - Submit dispute through the app
   - Provide evidence (photos, messages)
   - Our team reviews within 48 hours
   - Fair resolution based on evidence

1. **Responding to Reviews**

## Safety and Security

### Personal Safety

1. **Meeting Clients/Workers**
   - Meet in public places when possible
   - Trust your instincts
   - Let someone know your location
   - Verify identity through the app

2. **On-Site Safety**
   - Follow proper safety procedures
   - Use appropriate protective equipment
   - Report unsafe conditions
   - Don't work in dangerous situations

### Data Security

1. **Account Security**
   - Use a strong, unique password
   - Enable two-factor authentication
   - Don't share login credentials
   - Log out on shared devices

2. **Privacy Protection**
   - We never share personal information
   - Payment details are encrypted
   - Control what information is visible
   - Report suspicious activity

### Reporting Issues

1. **Report Inappropriate Behavior**
   - Use the report function in profiles/messages
   - Contact support immediately for serious issues
   - We investigate all reports promptly
   - Your safety is our priority

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

1. **App Not Loading**
   - Check internet connection
   - Force close and restart app
   - Update to latest version
   - Restart your device

2. **Payment Issues**
   - Verify payment method is valid
   - Check bank account/card status
   - Contact your bank if needed
   - Reach out to our support team

3. **Notification Problems**
   - Check app notification settings
   - Verify phone notification permissions
   - Update app to latest version
   - Check "Do Not Disturb" settings

4. **Profile/KYC Issues**
   - Ensure documents are clear and valid
   - Check file size limits (10MB max)
   - Use accepted formats (JPG, PNG, PDF)
   - Contact support for verification status

### Getting Help

1. **In-App Help**
   - Tap "Help" in app settings
   - Browse FAQ section
   - Submit support ticket
   - Live chat (business hours)

2. **Self-Service Options**
   - Check your account settings
   - Review transaction history
   - Update payment methods
   - Manage notification preferences

## Contact Support

### Support Channels

1. **In-App Support**
   - Fastest response time
   - Access to your account details
   - Available 24/7 for urgent issues
   - Live chat during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST)

2. **Email Support**
   - support@handworkmarketplace.com
   - Response within 24 hours
   - Include your account email and issue details
   - Attach screenshots if helpful

3. **Phone Support**
   - 1-800-HANDWORK (1-800-426-3967)
   - Available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
   - For urgent issues only
   - Have your account information ready

### Before Contacting Support

1. **Try These First**
   - Check the FAQ section
   - Restart the app
   - Check your internet connection
   - Update to the latest app version

2. **Information to Include**
   - Your account email
   - Description of the issue
   - Steps you've already tried
   - Screenshots or error messages
   - Device type and app version

### Emergency Situations

For immediate safety concerns or emergencies:
- Call local emergency services (911)
- Then report the incident through the app
- Contact our emergency line: 1-800-URGENT-1
